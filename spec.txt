Description:
This extension adjusts photo and video size on instagram web page.

1. Resizes instagram news feed page (https://www.instagram.com/) //adjustPhotosFeed()

2. Optimizes photo and video sizes of carousel gallery. Changes navigation buttons positions. (e.g. https://www.instagram.com/kcdeane/ adjusts opened posts) //adjustPopupGallery()

3. Resizes single post size (e.g. https://www.instagram.com/p/BY9kcQFl2lK/?taken-by=kcdeane) //adjustSinglePagePhoto()
